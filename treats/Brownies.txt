185g butter
185g dark chocolate
85g plain flour
40g cocoa powder
3 large eggs
250g caster sugar
50g chocolate chips - white/milk/dark

-Preheat oven to 160C
-Grease an line a cake tin
-Melt chocolate and butter together 
-Add eggs and sugar into bowl, mix with whisk until lie a thick shake
-Fold in melted butter and chocolate
-Sieve flour, cocoa powder and gently fold into mix
-Stir in chocolate chips
-Pour mix into cake tin and smooth out the top
-Bake for 25 mins until firm on top, then cool on wire rack

-Notes
Can replace chocolate chips with fruit e.g. dried cranberries
