100g butter
100g dark chocolate
150g light brown soft sugar
3 large eggs
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
50g plain flour

-Preheat oven to 200C, butter 6 dariole moulds and place on baking tray
-Melt butter and chopped dark chocolate together and stir until smooth, put aside for 15 mins to cool
-Mix in light brown soft sugar, eggs one at a time, vanilla extract and flour, using whisk
-Divide mixture among 6 moulds
-Bake for 10-12 mins until tops are firm to touch but middles are squidgy
-Run a knife around the edge of each mould to get out, then turn out onto plate and serve with ice cream

-Notes
Can freeze or refridgerate mix after dividing into moulds. If cooking from frozen, bake for 16 mins
