50g butter
Cocoa powder for dusting
200g dark chocolate
200g butter
200g golden caster sugar
4 eggs and 4 yolks
200g plain flour

-Heavily brush melted butter over inside of all moulds, then place moulds in fridge or freezer. Brush more melted butter over chilled butter, add spoonfull of cocoa powder into mould and turn to cover the butter. Tap excess back into pot
-Melt chopped dark chocolate and cubed butter together and stir until smooth. Leave for 10 mins to cool
-Whisk 4 eggs and 4 yolks with golden caster sugar until thick, pale, and whisk leaves a trail
-Sift flour into eggs, beat together
-Pour melted chocolate into egg mixture in thirds, beating well between each. Beat mixture until combined to loose cake batter
-Divide mix between moulds. Chill for 20 mins
-Preheat oven to 200C
-Put moulds on baking tray, bake for 10-12 mins until tops form a crust and start to come away from sides of mould. Remove from oven, leave sit for 1 min then turn out and serve

-Notes
Can freeze mix for up to a month when in the moulds. To bake from frozen, bake for 15-17 mins
Goes well with ice cream and caramel sauce
