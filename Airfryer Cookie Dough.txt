60g unsalted butter
100g light brown sugar
1 egg
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
100g plain flour
25g cocoa powder
1/2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
75g white chocolate chips
75g milk chocolate chips

-Add butter to bowl with sugar and mix until combined
-Add egg and vanilla extract and mix
-Add flour, bicarbonate of soda, cocoa powder and chocolate chips, mix to dough
-Divide into ramekins, air fry on air fry mode at 160C for 11 mins for cooked top and gooey middle
-Remove from air fryer and leave to cool for 3 mins and serve with ice cream

-Notes
Can freeze in ramekins, bake from frozen by adding 1-2 mins air frying
Can swap light brown sugar for granulated sugar
