350g self-raising flour
1tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
85g butter
3tbsp caster sugar
175ml warm milk
1tsp vanilla extract
1 egg
Jam
Clotted cream

-Preheat oven to 220C
-Put flour, salt and baking powder into bowl, mix
-Add butter, cubed, and rub in with fingers until fine crumbs
-Stir in the caster sugar
-Heat milk in a jug for 30s until warm not hot, add vanilla extract and squeeze of lemon juice
-Put baking tray in the oven
-Make well in dry mix, add liquid mix and combine with knife
-Flour work surface, tip dough out. Cover dough and hands with flour and fold dough over 2-3 times
-Pat dough into round shape 4cm deep, cut into 5cm discs
-Brush dough with beaten egg, then put on baking tray
-Bake for 10 mins until risen and golden

-Notes
-Can freeze once cool. To eat, defrost then put in oven at 160C for few minutes
