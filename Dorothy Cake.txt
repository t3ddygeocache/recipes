325g plain chocolate
110g butter
Tin of condensed milk
285g rice tea biscuits
Marshmallows (optional)

-Melt half of the chocolate with the butter and condensed milk
-Crush biscuits and stir into the mix (and add marshmallow if applicable)
-Line a tin with aluminium foil, pack mix in and chill for 3 hours
-Melt remaining chocolate and spread over chilled mix
-Chill again in the fridge

-Notes
Also called fridge cake
