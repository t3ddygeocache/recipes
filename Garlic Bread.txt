-Dough
650g strong white flour + extra for dusting
7g dried yeast
2tsp salt
25ml olive oil
50ml warm milk
325ml warm water

-Toppings
Garlic salt
Garlic puree
Butter
Mozzarella

-Mix flour, yeast and salt in bowl and stir in olive oil and milk. Gradually add water, mixing to form soft dough
-Put dough on floured surface and knead for 5 mins until smooth and elastic. Move to clean bowl, cover with damp towel for 90 mins to rise, until it doubles in size
-Knock dough back, knead until smooth, roll into ball, set aside for 30-60 mins until risen again
-Preheat oven to highest setting
-Roll out dough on lightly floured surface
-Water down tomato puree and spread over the dough
-Add toppings, heavy on garlic salt and puree, then add mozzarella
-Bake for 10-12 minutes until cheese melts and base is crisp and golden brown around the edges

-Notes
Can freeze dough after proving, step before preheating oven. Defrost overnight in fridge and continue with recipe
